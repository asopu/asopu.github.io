Bio


Born, raised, and currently living in beautiful Eastern Washington with her growing family, Ariel finds joy in simple things.

Pursuing a BFA in Visual Communication Design, she seeks out challenges to improve her understanding and expand her artistic ablilities. Ariel is looking forward to graduation in Fall of 2016 and the opportunites that will follow.

Spending 4 years living and working on the island of Oahu, where she met her husband and welcomed the first of her two children, Ariel had an enriching exposure to cultures from all over the world.
